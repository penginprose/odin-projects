Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

This easy, lightning-fast fried rice is a great way to introduce kimchi to your family-- mixed with rice, spinach, and eggs, its spicy nature is tempered. Make sure your pan is thoroughly pre-heated before you add any ingredients. This will help keep the cook time quick, but also produces a proper fried rice.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/4 cup chopped kimchi, drained and 1/4 cup juice reserved
  • 8.8 ounces jasmine rice
  • 5 ounces fresh baby spinach
  • 1.5 tbsp reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tsps gochujang
  • 2 tsps sesame oil
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons sesame oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium high. Add green onions and garlic; saute 1 minute. Add chopped kimchi and Jasmine Rice; saute 4 minutes. Gradually add fresh spinach, stirring until spinach wilts, about 2 minutes. Stir in kimchi juice, soy sauce, and gochujang. Remove from heat and set aside.
  2. Heat remaining sesame oil in a medium nonstick skillet over medium heat. Carefully crack eggs into hot oil and fry until desired degree of doneness. Place 1 egg on top of 1 cup of fried rice and serve immediately.
